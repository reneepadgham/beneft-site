Break through the social media noise.

Beneft is a crowdsourcing platform for visual content. We connect brands in need of social media content with creators that need to build their portfolio.


Brands

Brands are non-profits and for-profits working on social good initiatives. Brands post campaigns based on their content needs.


Creators

Creators are designers. Most designers on our platform are upper-level design students, but we encourage designers of all types to join in and contribute.


Campaigns

A campaign is a project posted by a brand. We encourage you to outline your campaign needs but don't be afraid to give our creators artistic freedom. They always deliver.
